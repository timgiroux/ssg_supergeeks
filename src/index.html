Project 📚

GeekSurvey is a web application to provide programmers with opportunities to participate in Software Engineering research.

To read more about GeekSurvey, see the Capstone project proposal written by the project sponsors.


Team Information 😃😃😃😃

SuperGeeks is a team of Computer Science students at Northern Arizona University.

The members of SuperGeeks are Tim Giroux, Pengfei Liu, Kyle Austria, and Gustavo Valencia.


Project Sponsor 🔑

The sponsors of GeekSurvey are Marco Gerosa and Igor Steinmacher.

Dr. Gerosa and Dr. Steinmacher are distinguished Software Engineering researchers. They have found that there is a communication problem with recruiting participants for S.E. studies. This is the problem that GeekSurvey sets out to solve.


Technical Advisor 👩‍🎓

The technical advisor to SuperGeeks is Felicity H. Escarzaga. She recently earned a degree in Mechanical Engineering, and is currently working towards a PhD in Informatics.
